Hanger Steak Sandwich

The perfect sandwich for lunch

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • 6oz hanger steak
  • 12 Shishito Peppers
  • 2 gloves of garlic
  • 1/2 sherry vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Preperation

  1. Put a grill pan over medium heat. Season meat with salt and pepper and grill in pan for about 4 minutes on each side until the steak is cooked to medium. Remove and let rest.
  2. In a bowl, mix the peppers, garlic, and vinegar.
  3. To serve, slice steak and place on the bottom piece of bread. Add Shishito salsa and top with other half of baguette.
  4. Serve immediately!
