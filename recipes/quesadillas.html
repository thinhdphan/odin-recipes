Kimchi Quesdillas

A personal favorite choice from yours truly!

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Store bought kimchi
  • Shredded pepperjack cheese
  • Butter

Preperation

  1. Pan to medium heat
  2. Sautee the kimchi
  3. Take out the kimchi and let it sit while you continue
  4. Add 2 slices of thin butter onto the pan until its melted
  5. Place the flour tortilla flat on the pen
  6. lower the heat a little
  7. Add the kimchi first on the tortilla on one side
  8. Then add the shredded pepperjack cheese on the kimchi
  9. fold the torilla in half and flip after 2 mins
  10. Enjoy!
